Avernis

The faction known as Avernis reveres the mysterious deity Avernus, the Unknown God believed to be the harbinger of the Eclipse. To them, the darkness is a divine punishment for ancient transgressions. Their priests preach that only by honoring Avernus and enduring his wrath can the world hope to find salvation.

Gloomers

The Gloomers embrace the inevitable darkness. They prepare tirelessly for a world plunged into endless night, crafting underground farms bathed in torchlight and stone shelters carved deep beneath the earth. For them, survival is a sacred duty, a stubborn refusal to be swallowed by the night.

Vanguards

Sworn to protect the innocent, the Vanguards rise each Eclipse to face the monsters born of the shadows. Armed with steel and resolve, they seek to keep the darkness at bay, defending their homes and kin from the terrors that come with the absence of light.

Starfinders

The Starfinders are scholars and seers, those who study the heavens and the cycles of the Eclipse. Gifted in prophecy and calculation, they can predict when the next shadow will fall, though the length and depth of the darkness remain mysteries beyond their reach. To them, knowledge is the light that will one day end the cycle.

Flamewardens

The Flamewardens were formed by sorcerers, warriors, and travellers who have banded together, using their fear of the ever-encroaching darkness as a means to connect. They have discovered the art of the soul flame, which they use to ward off the shadows, and have settled high up in the tallest of the mountains, staying ever-vigilant, and closing the gates of their towns every time a whisper of the coming darkness appears.
