Dark Paladin Caelan Duskbane

“If I must wear the shadow to shield you from it, so be it.”

Once a devoted Light Paladin, Caelan’s fate changed when his sacred armor was touched by the void-hand of an Enderman while saving the Starfinder heir. Though his intentions were noble, the corruption left its mark. Shunned by his father and branded “Dark Paladin,” Caelan rose from loss and disgrace to become the resolute leader of the Vanguards. Beneath the obsidian steel and cold stare lies a protector who shoulders the weight of every soul he cannot save.

Lore of The Vanguards

The faction known as Avernis reveres the mysterious deity Avernus, the Unknown God believed to be the harbinger of the Eclipse. To them, the darkness is not a curse to be fought, but a divine punishment for sins long forgotten. Their high temples are shrouded in ash and silence, where the red-glass windows filter moonlight into crimson beams upon bloodstone altars. High Priest Malrik leads them with unwavering devotion, claiming visions from the darkness itself. His sermons echo with prophecy, warning of a final Eclipse — one that will never end — should the world stray too far from humility.

Some call them zealots, others saviors. But all who cross paths with Avernis know this: they fear no shadow, for they believe it is the hand of their god.
