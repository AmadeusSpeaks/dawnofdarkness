Eclipse: Shadow Over the Land

Every few weeks, the moon eclipses the sun, plunging Elaria into a prolonged night known simply as the Eclipse. Crops fail, monsters stir, and the balance of life teeters on the edge of collapse. Though natural in origin, few would call it anything but cursed.

The Five Paths

Elaria’s five great factions see the Eclipse through different eyes: Avernus sees divine wrath, Gloomers seek survival underground, Vanguards fight the beasts, Starfinders study the skies, and the Shadowborn exploit the chaos. Each walks their own path in the dark.

The Great Eclipse

A darkness lasting fourteen nights once swallowed the realm. Food vanished, faith faltered, and many perished. Now, all fear the day the Eclipse returns for longer than memory dares to hold.

The Book of Shadows

Whispers tell of a forbidden tome, lost to time, capable of calling forth night or banishing it. Many seek the Book, hoping to control the Eclipse—or become its master.
