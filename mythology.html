Avernus, The Unseen Judgment

Avernus is named by ancient prophecy as the unknown god behind the Eclipse. Whether divine punishment or cosmic retribution, his silence is feared more than his fury. His only prophet vanished before her vision was complete.

The Starfinders’ Watch

With obsidian telescopes and sky-maps etched in silver, the Starfinders watch the heavens. They predict the coming of each Eclipse—but even they admit they cannot see what lies on the other side of the shadow.

The Oracle’s Prophecy

“I saw the shadow before the sun was born. I dreamed of moons that drink the light of life, and woke screaming beneath a sky that did not end. The Eclipse is no accident. It is the eye of Avernus blinking— a silence older than gods, watching, waiting. When it opens fully, the stars shall fall like ash, and all who walk in light shall drown in dusk. One shall rise bearing fire not born of flame, and they alone may choose: To end the night… or become it.”
